About Me

Hello and welcome. My name is Baxter, and this blog serves the purpose of me relaying some tips and tricks about running from an amateur perspective I was a cross country and track runner for 4 years in high school, and a runner for leisure afterwards. I learned a lot about running during that time, and I would like to share some things I learned over the years. At the moment, I am a 19 year old college student studying game development with the hopes of working in the games industry someday. I am from Michigan, United States, and I have lived However my whole life, save for a 6 month period where I lived in Arizona about 10 years ago. I love sushi, frogs, and computers.
My personal hobbies include:

  • Gaming
  • Running
  • Reading
  • Programming

I hope while reading this blog, you can get a taste of one of my hobbies. Running, both competitively and casually, is something I have spent quite a bit of time on in my life so far so I feel very connected to the sport, and greatly enjoy discussing it.
